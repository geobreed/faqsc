---

aturannya ada di PER 25 th 2018, tanya dulu dia buat dgt utk badan atau op. di petunjuk pengisiannya tercantum : For individual, completes PART I and PART II of DGT Page 1, and PART IV and PART VII of DGT Page 2 For non individual other than mentioned in number 2, completes PART I and PART II of DGT Page 1, and PART V, PART VI, and PART VII of DGT Page 2
