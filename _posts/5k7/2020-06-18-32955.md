error marcos. cara setting macros di laporan realisasi

---

Silakan mencoba langkah berikut: 1. Klik tombol Microsoft Office, lalu klik Excel Options 2. Klik Trust Center 3. Klik Trust Center Settings 4. Klik Macro Setting, lalu pilih enabled all macros 5. Re open file Excelnya
